Things to do in Winter

There are many fun things to do in the Winter. Here is a list of activities to do in the Winter with your friends or family.

1. Build a snowman or snow fort

2. Go on a winter photo scavenger hunt

3. Go winter camping as a family and learn survival skills

4. Have a snowball fight

5. Hold a bonfire and invite friends over

6. Host a snow sculpture competition

7. Ice fishing

8. Ice skating
